Each Excel workbook contains two sheets:

Full MAED template with data rows populated and Excel formulas for derived rows.

📊 Sheet 2: Raw Data

Flat layout — variable names in Column A, years across columns:

  • Population (million) — World Bank SP.POP.TOTL
  • Total GDP (constant 2015 USD billion) — World Bank WDI
  • Urban Population (%) — World Bank WDI
  • Rural Population (%) — World Bank WDI
  • Active Labour Force (million) — ILO ILOSTAT
  • Household Size (persons/hh) — UN Population Division
  • Pop in Large Cities >300k (million) — UN DESA WUP 2018
  • Sectoral GDP — Agriculture (%) — World Bank WDI
  • Sectoral GDP — Industry (%) — World Bank WDI
  • Sectoral GDP — Manufacturing (%) — World Bank WDI
  • Sectoral GDP — Services (%) — World Bank WDI
