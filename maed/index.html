What’s in the download?

Each Excel workbook contains two sheets:

📋 Sheet 1: Socio-Economic

Full MAED template with data rows populated and Excel formulas for all derived/calculated rows (growth rates, per capita, household counts, sectoral values in USD).

📊 Sheet 2: Raw Data

Clean flat layout — variable names in Column A, years 2010–2023 across columns. All input variables in one place:

  • Population (million) — World Bank SP.POP.TOTL
  • Total GDP (constant 2015 USD billion) — World Bank WDI
  • Urban Population (%) — World Bank WDI
  • Rural Population (%) — World Bank WDI
  • Active Labour Force (million) — ILO ILOSTAT
  • Household Size (persons/household) — UN Population Division
  • Population in Large Cities >300k (million) — UN DESA WUP 2018
  • Sectoral GDP — Agriculture (%) — World Bank WDI
  • Sectoral GDP — Industry (incl. construction, %) — World Bank WDI
  • Sectoral GDP — Manufacturing (%) — World Bank WDI
  • Sectoral GDP — Services (%) — World Bank WDI
