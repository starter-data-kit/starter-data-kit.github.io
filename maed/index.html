What’s in the download?

Each workbook has two sheets: Socio-Economic (template with formulas) and Raw Data (flat layout):

  • Population (million) — World Bank
  • Total GDP (constant 2015 USD billion) — World Bank
  • Urban Population (%) — World Bank
  • Rural Population (%) — World Bank
  • Active Labour Force (million) — ILO
  • Household Size (persons/hh) — UN
  • Pop in Large Cities >300k (million) — UN DESA
  • Sectoral GDP — Agriculture, Industry, Manufacturing, Services (%) — World Bank
