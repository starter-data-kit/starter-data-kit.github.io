OSeMOSYS

Open Source Energy Modelling System

Downloads 1_SDK_<Country>.xlsx directly from Google Drive.

Each SDK contains a total of 12 parameters, which are described below. Likewise, each SDK includes 199 technologies and 30 useful energy demands distributed across the different sectors mentioned previously.

The design of these SDKs is intended to support the modelling of whole energy systems at the country level, enabling comprehensive sector-coupling analyses. However, users may choose to use the complete dataset or only selected components, depending on their specific needs.

Parameter
Description
Annual demand
Historical and projected useful energy demand 2023–2050.
Capacity factor
Ratio of energy produced by a generating unit for the period considered to the energy that could have been produced at continuous full operation during the same period. Applicable to renewable power technologies only.
Availability factor
Planned and forced outage percentage.
Operational life
Technical lifespan.
Installed capacity
Historical installed capacity per technology.
Efficiency
Ratio between output energy and input energy.
Capital cost
Overnight costs from 2023 to 2050. Projected data was considered when available, otherwise constant values were assumed.
Fixed cost
Fixed operational and maintenance costs. Projected data was considered when available, otherwise constant values were assumed.
Variable cost
Variable costs represent the fuel costs in the case of primary energy supply and import technologies, and the variable non-fuel costs for the rest of the technologies.
Emission factor
Dioxide carbon emission factors per fuel.
Fossil fuel reserves
Crude oil, natural gas, coal, and uranium reserves.
Renewable energy potential
Estimated maximum potential for power generation from renewable sources.
